Company History

Olive Agricultural Technology Company was founded in 2018 to address the high cost of olive harvesting and the poor quality of oil produced from traditional harvesting and storage methods in Middle Eastern countries. Representatives of the company visited the leading olive oil-producing countries and imported technologies and tools to solve these issues. The company stood out in the Jordanian market by selling imported olive harvesting machines from European countries. It also expanded its operations to include organizational and administrative work for investment olive farms, facing significant challenges in these farms and other crops, primarily related to labor and organizational issues affecting production efficiency reliant on advanced technology software. Our company currently serves a broad sector, including all stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain, with a primary focus on serving farmers. Our current strength lies in a vast database comprising all farmers in Jordan, along with engineers and workers in this sector.
