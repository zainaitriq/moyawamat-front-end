Revolutionizing Olive Harvesting: How Technology is Transforming the Middle Eastern Olive Industry

08 Jan 2024

The olive industry has long been a cornerstone of agricultural production in the Middle East, particularly in countries like Jordan, where olives and olive oil hold significant cultural, economic, and environmental importance. However, traditional methods of olive harvesting, which rely heavily on manual labor, have posed challenges to both productivity and the quality of the olive oil produced. These challenges include high labor costs, slow harvesting processes, and oil degradation due to poor storage methods.

Recognizing these issues, the Olive Agricultural Technology Company (OilTec) was founded in 2018 with the mission of revolutionizing olive harvesting in the region. OilTec aims to address the inefficiencies of traditional methods by introducing modern technologies that are transforming the industry.

OilTec continues to be at the forefront of innovation in the Middle Eastern olive industry. Beyond importing and distributing advanced harvesting machines, the company also provides training and support to farmers, ensuring that they can make the most of these new technologies. By attending international agricultural expos and collaborating with global experts, OilTec ensures that the latest advancements are made accessible to local farmers.
